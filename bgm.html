About the Division

The cleaning and facility-services specialist within RCC-BGM

Brilliant General Maintenance provides recurring and specialized facility services for commercial properties. Programs can be structured around daily, weekly, monthly, project-based or site-specific requirements, with tasks, frequencies, access conditions and quality-review methods defined before service begins.

Reliable Concepts Corporation remains the construction and building-improvement division, while Brilliant focuses on cleanliness, presentation, specialty environments, sustainability and connected facility workflows.

  • Recurring janitorial programs for offices, common areas and employee spaces
  • Specialty cleaning for cleanrooms, labs and controlled environments
  • Floor, carpet and upholstery maintenance plans
  • Interior and selected exterior window services
  • Green-cleaning consultation and waste-diversion support
  • Robotics, occupancy sensors and connected cleaning dashboards

How a BGM Program Is Developed

A clear five-step process from initial walkthrough to ongoing refinement.

01

Walkthrough

Review the property, service zones, current condition, access and operating schedule.

02

Scope Development

Define tasks, frequencies, materials, equipment, exclusions and customer responsibilities.

03

Program Planning

Confirm staffing windows, supervision, security procedures and communication routes.

04

Launch

Orient the team, establish checklists and begin the approved service schedule.

05

Review

Inspect performance, address issues and refine the program through agreed changes.

Common Questions

What does Brilliant General Maintenance provide?

Commercial janitorial, specialty cleaning, floor and window care, sustainability support and connected cleaning services for qualified properties.

Can the janitorial schedule be customized?

Yes. Frequency and service windows should be based on the property, occupancy, traffic and approved scope.

Does BGM provide cleanroom cleaning?

Specialized programs may be assessed, but room class, SOPs, validation and compliance responsibilities must be confirmed.

Can BGM provide robotics and sensors?

Connected technology may be considered for suitable facilities and confirmed equipment deployments.

Does BGM serve multiple locations?

Multi-site programs may be considered based on active markets, local resources and the required service standard.

Can BGM coordinate with RCC construction work?

Yes. Post-construction cleaning or ongoing service may be separately coordinated after the responsible project scope is ready.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing depends on area, frequency, conditions, labor windows, equipment, specialty requirements and location.

What is the first step?

Request a walkthrough with the property location, facility type, approximate size, schedule and service priorities.

Ready to build a cleaning program?

Request a Brilliant General Maintenance Walkthrough

Share the property type, location, approximate size, operating schedule and cleaning priorities. Brilliant can review the facility and develop a recurring, specialty or technology-supported service plan.
