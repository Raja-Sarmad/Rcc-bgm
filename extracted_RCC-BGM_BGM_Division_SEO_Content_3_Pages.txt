1. Page Purpose and Keyword Strategy
The BGM page is a division hub. It should explain who Brilliant is, summarize its service families and route users to detailed pages. It should
not attempt to outrank the dedicated Commercial Janitorial, Commercial Cleaning, Cleanroom Cleaning, Industrial Cleaning, Deep Cleaning or

commercial cleaning and facility services, specialty commercial cleaning, technology-driven facility management and commercial
cleaning company. Exact service phrases should appear briefly and link to their dedicated landing pages.

5. Floor, Carpet and Upholstery Care
High-traffic commercial surfaces require maintenance plans based on material, condition, traffic, cleaning history and manufacturer requirements. Brilliant
can assess approved carpet, upholstery and hard-floor needs as recurring maintenance or a separate restorative scope.
• Commercial carpet extraction and spot-treatment planning
• Approved upholstery and fabric-surface cleaning
• Hard-floor cleaning, burnishing and periodic maintenance
• Floor stripping, sealing and waxing where material-appropriate
• Transition planning between restorative work and recurring janitorial care
• Drying time, access and slip-risk communication

6. Window Cleaning
Commercial window cleaning should be qualified by the building, glass condition, interior access, exterior height, anchorage, equipment, surrounding
pedestrian activity and local safety requirements. The page should not promise service at every height or building type without market-specific
confirmation.
• Interior glass, partitions and entrance doors
• Exterior windows at approved and accessible elevations
• Frames, sills and surrounding finish areas when included
• Service scheduling around tenants, customers and building access
• Separate qualification for lifts, suspended access and high-rise conditions

janitorial work. Brilliant's public service positioning includes cleanroom services, construction cleanups and ESD floor services; exact protocols and room
classifications must be confirmed before publication or proposal.
Cleanroom support

Customer SOPs, gowning, contamination controls, approved materials and documentation requirements.

Laboratory cleaning

ESD floor services

Maintenance methods selected for the installed flooring system and customer technical requirements.

Construction cleanup

work.
