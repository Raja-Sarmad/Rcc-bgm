2. Who RCC-BGM Is
RCC-BGM represents two companies serving commercial facilities from different
but connected operational perspectives. Reliable Concepts Corporation focuses
on construction and building improvement. Brilliant General Maintenance focuses
on janitorial, specialty cleaning and technology-supported facility services.
The shared platform helps customers understand both companies through one
website while keeping the legal entities, service responsibilities and trade
qualifications clear.
• Reliable Concepts Corporation: commercial construction, tenant improvements
and building services.
• Brilliant General Maintenance: commercial janitorial, specialty cleaning and
connected facility support.
• Combined position: one relationship for properties that may need both
project-based and recurring services.
• Commercial focus: offices, technology, life sciences, healthcare, industrial and
other approved business environments.
