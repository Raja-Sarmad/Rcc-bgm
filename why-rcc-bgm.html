Common questions

Why choose RCC-BGM?

RCC-BGM connects two commercial facility companies so customers can access cleaning, maintenance, HVAC, MEP and construction capabilities through a more coordinated relationship.

Are RCC and BGM the same company?

They are presented as two independent companies working in concert: Reliable Concepts Corporation and Brilliant General Maintenance.

Can we hire only one division?

Yes. A customer may request one focused RCC or BGM service without purchasing a wider facility program.

Can RCC-BGM work in occupied commercial buildings?

Services and projects may be phased around approved operating hours, access restrictions, tenants, employees and visitors.

Does one team perform every service?

Not necessarily. Work should remain with the appropriate company, licensed trade or specialist, with responsibilities documented in the scope.

Does RCC-BGM support multiple locations?

Multi-location programs may be considered based on active markets, local teams, licensing and the required scope.

How do we start?

Request a facility assessment with the property type, location, required services, operating schedule, priority areas and preferred timeline.
