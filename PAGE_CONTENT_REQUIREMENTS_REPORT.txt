SECTION 7: CONVERSION STRATEGY
================================================================================

THREE-TIER CTA APPROACH:
Primary CTAs: "Request a [Service] Assessment/Quote"
Secondary CTAs: "Explore [Division/Service] Services" or "Schedule a Walkthrough"
Confirmation CTAs: "Confirm My Location" or similar

LEAD QUALIFICATION PROCESS:
1. User identifies property type/location
2. User selects service need or requests assessment
3. Form submission with required qualification fields
4. Automated email confirmation and internal routing
5. Representative reviews and schedules site visit/phone call
6. Scope is defined and proposal provided

MULTI-LOCATION SUPPORT:
Website supports multi-site programs for customers with portfolios, but each location
requires individual scope and service verification. Form includes field for number of
buildings/locations.
