Commercial HVAC services FAQs

What commercial HVAC services does RCC-BGM provide?

Commercial HVAC installation, maintenance, repair, equipment replacement and project coordination, subject to location and approved scope.

Do you provide HVAC maintenance services?

Yes. Maintenance tasks and frequency should be confirmed for the facility and equipment.

Do you repair commercial HVAC systems?

Yes. Repair scope depends on system, issue and service location.

Can you install or replace commercial HVAC equipment?

New installation and replacement may be planned after reviewing the existing system, building requirements and project conditions.

Do you provide emergency HVAC repair?

Time-sensitive requests can be reviewed, but hours, availability and response time must be confirmed for the market.

Can HVAC work be included in tenant improvements?

Yes. HVAC can be coordinated with tenant-improvement, renovation and construction requirements.

Can HVAC work happen in an occupied building?

Work may be phased or scheduled around facility operations depending on access and project requirements.

Do you service every equipment brand?

Brand-specific capability should be confirmed before claiming universal manufacturer coverage.

Is a site walk required?

A site walk or system assessment is recommended for accurate scope and pricing.
