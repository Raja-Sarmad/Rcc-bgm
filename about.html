Who we are

Built around the commercial facilities our clients depend on

RCC-BGM represents two companies serving commercial facilities from different but connected operational perspectives. The shared platform helps customers understand both companies through one website while keeping the legal entities, service responsibilities and trade qualifications clear.

  • Maintenance construction and corrective building repairs
  • Commercial HVAC installation, repair and maintenance
  • MEP coordination for mechanical, electrical and plumbing
  • Commercial carpentry, painting and landscaping
  • Facility upgrades and modernization projects

Why the two-company structure matters

A commercial property may require daily cleaning, seasonal floor care, an HVAC replacement, tenant repairs and a later build-out. The RCC-BGM structure allows these requirements to be introduced through one platform without pretending that they are the same service.

Operating principles that guide every engagement

RCC-BGM operates with clear accountability, practical planning and continuous improvement across every project and service program.

Accountability

Define who owns each scope, approval, change and completion item before work begins.

Commercial Practicality

Plan around occupancy, schedules, access, business continuity and the actual building conditions.

Quality Control

Use inspections, checklists, milestones and review points that match the service or project scope.

Clear Communication

Establish authorized contacts, reporting methods and escalation routes for every engagement.

Continuous Improvement

Use service reviews, facility observations and completed-project findings to identify future priorities.

Responsible Qualification

Confirm licensing, specialist trades, regulated services and technical responsibilities before work begins.

Frequently asked questions

What is RCC-BGM?

RCC-BGM is the connected website and facility-services platform for Reliable Concepts Corporation and Brilliant General Maintenance, supporting commercial properties through construction, cleaning and ongoing facility programs.

What does Reliable Concepts Corporation do?

Reliable focuses on commercial construction, tenant improvements, maintenance construction, HVAC, MEP coordination and approved building-improvement services.

What does Brilliant General Maintenance do?

Brilliant focuses on commercial janitorial, specialty cleaning, quality-control systems and technology-supported facility services for recurring programs.

Are Reliable and Brilliant the same company?

They are presented as separate companies that work in concert through the RCC-BGM platform. Each maintains its own legal entity, service responsibilities and trade qualifications.

Can a customer hire only one company?

Yes. Customers may request one focused Reliable or Brilliant service without purchasing a combined program. The two-company structure supports both standalone and coordinated engagements.

Can the companies coordinate on one property?

Yes. Related scopes may be coordinated when cleaning, construction, HVAC, maintenance or facility upgrades affect the same building or schedule.

What industries does RCC-BGM serve?

The platform highlights technology, office, life-science and healthcare environments and also supports other qualified commercial property types including industrial, retail and hospitality facilities.

Where does RCC-BGM operate?

The website lists primary hubs across multiple U.S. states including Arizona, California, Florida, Illinois, Michigan, Nevada, New Mexico, New York, Tennessee, Texas and Utah. Exact availability must be confirmed by location, company and service.

How do we start?

Contact RCC-BGM with the property type, location, current requirement, operating schedule and preferred timeline. The team can then determine the right next conversation.
