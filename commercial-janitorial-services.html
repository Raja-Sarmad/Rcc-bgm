Recurring service logic

The right frequency for each task

A janitorial program should separate high-frequency responsibilities from tasks that only require periodic attention. The schedule should reflect how the property is actually used.

01

Daily tasks

Restrooms, break rooms, waste stations, entrances and shared areas where activity requires daily attention.

02

Multiple service days

Active facilities may need several visits per week without a full daily program.

03

Weekly tasks

Lower-use areas, selected offices and detailed responsibilities can follow a weekly schedule.

04

Periodic support

Floor, surface and event-based tasks can be scheduled around condition and facility activity.

Questions from facility teams

Commercial janitorial services FAQs

What are commercial janitorial services?

Recurring cleaning and facility-care tasks for offices, commercial buildings and business properties, commonly including restrooms, waste, common areas, offices and routine floors.

How often can service be scheduled?

Programs may be scheduled daily, several times per week, weekly or at another approved recurring frequency.

Do you provide commercial building janitorial services?

Yes. RCC-BGM can develop programs for office buildings, multi-tenant properties and other commercial facilities.

Can service happen after business hours?

Schedules may be coordinated before or after normal operating hours depending on access and local availability.

What is the difference between janitorial and commercial cleaning?

Janitorial service usually means recurring daily or scheduled tasks. Commercial cleaning may also include periodic, deep or specialized work.

Can the checklist be customized?

Yes. Areas, frequencies and responsibilities can be tailored according to the facility.

Do you provide daytime porter services?

Daytime on-site support should be confirmed according to facility location, staffing requirements and requested scope.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is affected by size, frequency, restrooms, common areas, floor types, service hours, waste volume and the required task scope.

How do we request a quote?

Provide the property address, approximate size, service frequency and facility information. RCC-BGM can arrange a walkthrough and prepare a proposal.
