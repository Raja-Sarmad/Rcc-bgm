2. The RCC-BGM Difference
Commercial buildings rarely have isolated needs. A tenant improvement may
affect HVAC, power, plumbing, carpentry, painting and final cleaning. A janitorial
program may reveal repair or maintenance needs. An equipment replacement
may require access, restoration and occupant communication.
RCC-BGM does not need to present every service as one universal package. The
stronger positioning is that two specialized companies can work in concert,
allowing related requirements to be reviewed around one property, one operating
environment and one wider facility plan.

12. Frequently Asked Questions
Why choose RCC-BGM?
RCC-BGM connects two commercial facility companies so customers can access
cleaning, maintenance, HVAC, MEP and construction capabilities through a more
coordinated relationship.

Are RCC and BGM the same company?
They are presented as two independent companies working in concert: Reliable
Concepts Corporation and Brilliant General Maintenance.

Can we hire only one division?
Yes. A customer may request one focused RCC or BGM service without purchasing a
wider facility program.

Tenant improvement

Coordinate construction, HVAC, MEP, carpentry and painting before
separately scoped final cleaning and occupancy preparation.

HVAC replacement

Review power, controls, drainage, access and finish restoration
rather than treating the equipment replacement as an isolated task.

Tenant move-out

Assess repairs, carpentry, painting, deep cleaning and the
difference between restoration and a new tenant build-out.

Related services may be coordinated where they affect the same building, schedule or
project outcome.
