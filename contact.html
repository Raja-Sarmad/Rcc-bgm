Frequently Asked Questions

What services can I request?

Commercial cleaning, janitorial, maintenance, HVAC, MEP, construction, tenant improvements, facility upgrades and other listed capabilities.

Do I need to know the exact service?

No. Select the unsure or multi-service option and describe the facility condition and required outcome.

Can I request multiple locations?

Yes. Include the number of properties, cities, required consistency and whether the scope is the same at each site.

Can I attach plans or photographs?

Approved file types may be uploaded. Do not submit protected health, payment-card or security-sensitive information.

Will I receive pricing immediately?

Pricing follows review of the property, access, conditions, location and final scope.

Can I use the form for an emergency?

The general form should not be used as an emergency channel. For life-safety, fire, medical, utility or other immediate emergencies, contact the appropriate emergency authority or utility provider.

What if my city is not listed?

Submit the location for coverage review before service availability is promised.

Can I contact RCC-BGM directly?

Yes. Call (844) 271-6677 or email info@rcc-bgm.com for direct enquiries.
