1. Page Purpose and Search Intent
The Contact page is primarily a conversion page. It should reduce uncertainty, collect the details needed for an informed first response and
route the visitor to the appropriate RCC, BGM or coordinated service path. It should not duplicate full service-page content or attempt to rank
independently for every cleaning, HVAC and construction keyword.
Natural supporting phrases can include contact RCC-BGM, request a facility assessment, commercial cleaning quote, commercial
construction consultation, facility maintenance proposal and commercial HVAC service enquiry. These phrases should appear in
descriptive labels and routing copy rather than repetitive SEO paragraphs.

2. Recommended Introductory Copy
Every commercial facility enquiry begins differently. Some properties need recurring janitorial service. Others need an HVAC assessment,
tenant improvement, corrective construction or a coordinated plan involving several services. Complete the form with the information currently
available. RCC-BGM can review the property, location and required outcome before confirming the next step.
For life-safety, fire, medical, utility or other immediate emergencies, contact the appropriate emergency authority or utility provider. The
general website form should not be described as an emergency-response channel unless RCC-BGM verifies a separate emergency service
and response process.

FORM QUALIFICATION, CONDITIONAL LOGIC AND RESPONSE
COPY
The form should be detailed enough to support a useful first review, but short enough that qualified visitors complete it. Conditional fields should appear only when
relevant.

6. Optional Qualification Fields
• Approximate square footage: useful for cleaning, painting, construction and multi-area projects.
• Number of buildings or locations: separates single-site and portfolio enquiries.
• Occupancy and operating hours: include shifts, visitors, customer hours and access restrictions.
• Required frequency: one-time, daily, weekly, recurring, project-based or unsure.
• Preferred timeline: immediate review, within 30 days, 1-3 months, 3-6 months or planning stage.
• Current condition or provider: optional context, not a required competitor-comparison field.
• Budget range: optional and only useful if the sales team has a defined qualification process.
• Preferred contact method: phone, email or scheduled walkthrough.
• File upload: photographs, plans, scope documents, equipment information or service records.

7. Conditional Form Logic
Cleaning

Frequency, service areas, shifts, current condition, approximate size and specialty requirements.

HVAC

Equipment type if known, symptoms, affected area, operational impact and available service records.

Construction

Project type, drawings, occupancy, completion target, landlord requirements and permit status if known.

Tenant improvement

Suite size, intended use, current condition, lease dates, drawings and required occupancy date.

Multi-service

Allow a connected description without forcing the visitor to complete every specialist field.

8. Enquiry Routing Rules
• BGM route: recurring cleaning, janitorial, floor care, specialty cleaning and approved robotics enquiries.
• RCC route: construction, tenant improvements, HVAC, MEP, carpentry, painting and facility upgrades.
• Joint review: cleaning plus construction, ongoing service after a project or unclear multi-trade needs.
• Location review: enquiries outside a listed market or involving location-specific licensing and resources.
• Specialist review: regulated, hazardous, engineered or compliance-sensitive work before a commitment is made.

FAQS, ACCESSIBILITY, SECURITY AND TECHNICAL SEO
The Contact page should be easy to understand, secure to submit and measurable without exposing personal or project information.

14. Frequently Asked Questions
What services can I request?

Commercial cleaning, janitorial, maintenance, HVAC, MEP, construction, tenant improvements, facility
upgrades and other listed capabilities.

Do I need to know the exact service?

No. Select the unsure or multi-service option and describe the facility condition and required outcome.

Can I request multiple locations?

Yes. Include the number of properties, cities, required consistency and whether the scope is the same
at each site.

Can I attach plans or photographs?

Approved file types may be uploaded. Do not submit protected health, payment-card or
security-sensitive information.

Will I receive pricing immediately?

Pricing should follow review of the property, access, conditions, location and final scope.

Can I use the form for an emergency?

The general form should not be presented as an emergency channel.

What if my city is not listed?

Submit the location for coverage review before service availability is promised.

Can I contact RCC-BGM directly?

The public website currently lists (844) 271-6677 and info@rcc-bgm.com.

15. Accessibility Requirements
• Use visible labels; placeholders must not replace field labels
• Associate validation messages with the correct inputs
• Support keyboard navigation and visible focus states
• Use appropriate input types and autocomplete attributes
• Do not rely only on color for errors or required fields
• Announce errors and successful submission to assistive technology
• Use mobile-friendly tap targets and readable text
• Let users correct errors without losing completed fields

16. Security and Privacy Controls
• Use HTTPS, server-side validation, CSRF protection and rate limiting
• Apply bot protection without blocking legitimate keyboard or assistive-technology users
• Validate file types, sizes and content; store uploads outside the public web root
• Escape and sanitize submitted text before displaying or emailing it
• Do not include submitted names, emails, phone numbers, addresses or descriptions in analytics
• Publish a privacy-policy link and define data retention and access controls
• Restrict CRM, upload and mailbox access according to staff responsibilities

17. Analytics and Conversion Tracking
Primary event

Successful facility-enquiry submission.

Secondary events

Phone click, email click, file upload, service selection and location click.

Quality dimensions

Service category, market, facility type and single-site versus multi-site.

Attribution

Preserve approved UTM and referrer data in the CRM record.

Privacy boundary

Never send personally identifiable or free-text project details to analytics tools.

18. SEO, Schema and Developer Notes
• Schema: ContactPage + BreadcrumbList + Organization.
• Organization contactPoint: include the current telephone and email only after confirmation.
• Breadcrumb: Home > Contact.
• Canonical: https://rcc-bgm.com/contact.
• Internal links: Services, Industries, Why RCC-BGM, About, Locations, RCC, BGM and relevant service pages.
• Indexing: keep Contact indexable; use noindex for a private form-success page.
• Performance: avoid heavy maps, background video or third-party widgets that delay the form.
• Mobile layout: place the form before secondary content and use a single-column field layout.

19. Claims and Details to Verify
Response-time promises; 24/7 emergency availability; office hours; physical office or headquarters addresses; support@ versus info@ email usage; department extensions;
service availability in every listed state; free-assessment claims; proposal timelines; consent requirements; privacy-policy language; file-upload security workflow; and
whether calls are recorded.
