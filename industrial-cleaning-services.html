Industrial facilities require a different approach

Standard office cleaning is not enough for an operational property

Manufacturing and industrial properties often combine production areas, warehouses, offices, employee spaces, loading zones and shared facilities within one site.

These environments may operate across shifts, contain restricted work zones and experience higher floor traffic, dust, packaging debris and operational activity than a standard office building. The scope should distinguish administrative, employee, warehouse and approved operational areas.

One property. Multiple operating zones.

Separating responsibilities across the industrial facility

Industrial properties contain spaces with different cleaning, access and scheduling requirements. Dividing the site into clear zones makes responsibilities and frequencies easier to understand.

  • Administrative: offices, reception and training
  • Employee support: kitchens, restrooms and entrances
  • Warehouse: approved floors, walkways and staging
  • Production-adjacent: approved non-sensitive areas
  • Restricted or excluded zones documented upfront

Questions from operations teams

Industrial cleaning services FAQs

What are industrial cleaning services?

Customized cleaning programs for manufacturing facilities, warehouses, production buildings and other operational properties.

What areas can RCC-BGM clean?

Service may include offices, employee areas, restrooms, break rooms, warehouses, approved floors, common areas and selected operational spaces.

Do you provide manufacturing and warehouse cleaning?

RCC-BGM may support approved areas within manufacturing and warehouse facilities after a site assessment confirms the scope.

Can cleaning be scheduled around production shifts?

Schedules can be reviewed around production, loading, employee shifts and approved access requirements.

Do you provide industrial floor cleaning?

Floor cleaning may be included depending on surface, condition, traffic and the required method.

Do you clean machinery or hazardous materials?

Specialist machinery, hazardous-material, confined-space and similar work must be separately reviewed and confirmed.

Can you provide daily industrial cleaning?

Daily service may be available depending on location, staffing and facility scope.

Is a site walkthrough required?

A walkthrough is strongly recommended because industrial layouts, access restrictions and operating conditions vary significantly.

What information is needed for a quote?

Provide the facility address, size, property type, operating shifts, service areas and known access or safety restrictions.
