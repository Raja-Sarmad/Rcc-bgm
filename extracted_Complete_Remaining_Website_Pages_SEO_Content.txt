Keyword Planner Interpretation
The supplied Google Keyword Planner ranges are broad market estimates. Close variants can share the same range
and must not be added together as separate demand. Low, medium and high competition refer to advertiser
competition, not organic SEO difficulty. Bid ranges indicate commercial advertiser value, not guaranteed lead value.
