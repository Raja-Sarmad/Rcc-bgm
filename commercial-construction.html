Questions from property and project teams

Commercial construction FAQs

What commercial construction services does RCC-BGM provide?

Commercial renovation, tenant improvement, maintenance construction, facility upgrades and approved building-system coordination.

Do you provide commercial building renovations?

Yes. Existing building renovations and commercial facility improvements can be assessed within the project scope.

Do you provide tenant build-outs?

Tenant build-outs and tenant improvements form part of RCC-BGM’s construction positioning, subject to project and market review.

Can you renovate occupied buildings?

Projects may be phased around tenants, employees, customers and access requirements.

Can HVAC and MEP be coordinated?

Approved HVAC and MEP requirements may be coordinated with construction and tenant improvements.

Do you provide carpentry and painting?

Selected carpentry, painting and finish work can be included within an approved project scope.

Do you perform ground-up construction?

Ground-up construction should not be assumed; project type, licensing and current capability must be confirmed.

Can you manage permits or engineering?

Permit, architecture and engineering responsibilities vary by project and jurisdiction and must be confirmed.

What information is needed for a proposal?

Provide address, objective, approximate area, timeline, occupancy, available plans, photos and known constraints.
