Cleaning designed around your operation

Your facility should not be forced into a standard cleaning package

Every commercial property operates differently. An office may need recurring workspace, restroom and common-area cleaning, while a manufacturing or technology facility may need carefully coordinated service around active work areas and restricted spaces.

RCC-BGM reviews how your building functions, which areas require the most attention and when cleaning can be performed with the least disruption. The result is a defined scope, realistic frequency and shared expectation from the beginning.

Questions from facility teams

Commercial cleaning services FAQs

What are commercial cleaning services?

Professional cleaning programs developed for offices, commercial buildings, retail properties, industrial facilities and other business environments. The scope may include routine cleaning, floor care, restrooms, shared spaces, waste collection and periodic deep cleaning.

What is normally included in a commercial cleaning program?

The service may include offices, workstations, common areas, restrooms, break rooms, floors, selected glass, waste removal and other approved tasks. The exact scope depends on the property.

How often should a commercial building be cleaned?

Frequency depends on occupancy, facility usage, property type and service expectations. Some buildings require daily cleaning while others use a customized recurring schedule.

Does RCC-BGM provide recurring commercial cleaning?

Yes. Programs can be developed around daily, recurring and periodic cleaning requirements, subject to location and service availability.

Can cleaning be scheduled after business hours?

Service timing can be coordinated around approved access and normal operations, depending on location and scope.

Do you provide commercial building and industrial cleaning?

RCC-BGM can develop programs for office buildings, shared facilities and selected industrial environments. The exact scope is confirmed during assessment.

Can the cleaning checklist be customized?

Yes. The final scope can be tailored to the building layout, cleaning priorities, frequency and operational requirements.

How can we receive a quote?

Submit the location, facility type, approximate size, preferred frequency and basic cleaning needs. RCC-BGM can arrange a review and prepare a customized proposal.
