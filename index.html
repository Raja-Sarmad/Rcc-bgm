Frequently Asked Questions

What services does RCC-BGM provide?

RCC-BGM provides integrated commercial cleaning, janitorial service, construction, tenant improvements, HVAC, MEP, carpentry, painting, landscaping, and specialized facility maintenance.

Can you manage both construction and cleaning?

Yes. RCC-BGM combines the construction and building system capabilities of Reliable Concepts Corporation with the janitorial and specialized cleaning expertise of Brilliant General Maintenance under a single managed relationship.

Do you serve medical and lab facilities?

Yes, we provide specialized ISO-compliant cleaning for cleanrooms, research laboratories, medical offices, life-science facilities, and healthcare environments.

Do you provide commercial HVAC maintenance?

Yes, we provide commercial HVAC installation, repair, preventive maintenance, and systems coordination alongside MEP services.
