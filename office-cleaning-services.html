Questions from workplace teams

Office cleaning services FAQs

What do office cleaning services include?

Office cleaning may include workstations, private offices, meeting rooms, reception areas, kitchens, break rooms, restrooms, floors, common areas and waste collection. The scope is customized for each workplace.

How often should an office be cleaned?

Frequency depends on office size, occupancy, visitors and shared-space usage. Active offices may require daily service, while smaller workplaces may use a less frequent schedule.

Does RCC-BGM provide recurring office cleaning?

Yes. Programs can be developed for daily, several-times-per-week, weekly and customized recurring schedules.

Can office cleaning happen after business hours?

Before-hours or after-hours cleaning may be available depending on access, location and the approved schedule.

Can the office checklist be customized?

Yes. The scope can be developed around your layout, employee activity and cleaning priorities.

Do you clean executive offices and restricted areas?

Restricted access can be discussed during onboarding and included only where authorized.

Do you offer office deep cleaning?

Periodic deep cleaning can be scheduled according to the workplace condition and required work.

Is office cleaning the same as janitorial service?

Office cleaning focuses on workplace areas, while janitorial service may include wider recurring building responsibilities. Both can be combined.

What information is needed for a quote?

Provide the office location, approximate size, employee count, preferred frequency, operating hours and priority areas.
