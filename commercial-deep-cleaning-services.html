When routine cleaning is not enough

Some facility areas require more than a standard recurring visit

Routine janitorial service maintains the ongoing condition of a property, but it may not address detailed buildup, low-frequency areas, heavily used surfaces, occupancy changes or post-project conditions.

Deep cleaning can be scheduled as a one-time project, added periodically to an existing program or planned around a specific building event. RCC-BGM starts with a walkthrough and organizes the work around condition, access and the available service window.

Understanding the service difference

Routine janitorial cleaning and deep cleaning serve different purposes

Routine janitorial service

Recurring responsibilities that maintain the day-to-day condition of a facility.

  • Waste and restroom care
  • Break rooms and offices
  • Common areas and routine floors

Commercial deep cleaning

Restroom and break-room deep cleaning

Restrooms and employee kitchens experience regular use and surface contact. Periodic detail can focus on surfaces and lower-frequency tasks outside the normal recurring checklist.

  • Fixtures, mirrors, counters and partitions
  • Floors, edges, doors and approved touchpoints
  • Counters, sinks, tables and eating areas
  • External appliance surfaces and waste stations

Sterilization, medical-grade disinfection, internal appliance cleaning and specialist food-service work require separate confirmation.

Questions from facility teams

Commercial deep cleaning services FAQs

What are commercial deep cleaning services?

More detailed cleaning than a normal recurring visit, addressing restrooms, kitchens, floors, common areas, glass, fixtures and other approved sections.

What is the difference from regular cleaning?

Regular cleaning maintains the building on a recurring schedule. Deep cleaning focuses on detailed, periodic or condition-based tasks.

Can RCC-BGM provide one-time deep cleaning?

One-time service may be available depending on location, property type and required scope.

Do you provide office deep cleaning?

Office deep cleaning may cover workspaces, meeting rooms, reception, kitchens, restrooms, floors and shared areas.

Can you clean commercial floors?

Floor cleaning may be included after reviewing material, condition and the required method.

Do you provide post-construction cleaning?

RCC-BGM can review cleaning after approved construction or improvement work. Heavy debris and specialist cleanup require separate confirmation.

Can deep cleaning happen after business hours?

After-hours service may be available depending on location, access and project duration.

Does deep cleaning include disinfection or sterilization?

Cleaning and disinfection are not automatically the same service. Methods, products and claims must be confirmed in the proposal.

Is a walkthrough required?

A walkthrough is strongly recommended because current condition directly affects scope and pricing.
